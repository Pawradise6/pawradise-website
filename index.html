Pawradise: The Paradise for Pets

A community-driven movement to help animals in need. Join the cause!

About the Project

Pawradise ($PAWX) is more than a memecoin; it's a bridge between our community and animal shelters. We created this project with the firm belief that cryptocurrencies can be a force for good. Our objective is simple yet powerful: to use the power of streaming and our community's spirit to fund animal shelters that work tirelessly to give animals a second chance.

With every interaction and contribution, Pawradise becomes a symbol of hope, providing vital resources for food, medical care, and a safe home for our four-legged friends. Together, we not only grow a token but also build a kinder world.

How It Works

Our mission comes to life through streaming on pump.fun and other platforms. Here's how we make a difference:

  • **Streaming:** Tune in to my live streams on pump.fun. During the broadcasts, we will introduce the project, share updates, and most importantly, have fun together!
  • **Active Community:** Every donation or interaction we receive on the stream, no matter the size, contributes directly to our fundraising goal.
  • **100% to the Cause:** We are committed to ensuring that **100%** of what is raised during the streams is donated to animal shelters.

Your participation is the force that fuels our mission. By joining, you become a hero for the animals.

Roadmap

Phase 1: Launch

Token launch on the blockchain, initial community creation on Telegram and Twitter/X, and activation of social media channels.

Phase 2: Expansion

Contacting content creators and streamers to integrate $PAWX donations, starting the first donations to animal shelters, and launching marketing campaigns.

Phase 3: Growth

Partnerships with large charitable organizations, developing a voting system so the community can decide which shelters to support, and listing on exchange platforms.
